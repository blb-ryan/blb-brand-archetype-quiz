**Q1.** Your brand's core promise to buyers is...
- A. Life can be simpler, more joyful, and full of good. `[Innocent]`
- B. We'll help you rise to any challenge and win. `[Hero]`
- C. We'll challenge the way things have always been done. `[Rebel]`
- D. We'll help you make sense of a complex world. `[Sage]`

**Q2.** When buyers think about your brand, they feel...

- B. Cared for, supported, and genuinely looked after. `[Caregiver]`

- D. Like something magical or transformational is possible. `[Magician]`

## Archetype Profiles
> These appear on the results screen and in the email. Edit the text freely — but do not change the archetype name in the `###` heading (e.g. `### Innocent`) as that is used as a key.

---

### Innocent
- **Display name:** The Innocent
- **Aliases:** Child · Optimist · Muse · Traditionalist
- **Motto:** "Life is good"
- **Description:** The Innocent doesn't overthink it. Life is simple, people are good, and things work out. This archetype radiates warmth and sincerity — and it connects because most people secretly want to believe that too.
- **Strengths:** Sweet, Charming, Sense of wonder, Cheerful, Playful, Warm-Hearted
- **Voice:** Simple, Concise, Cheerful, Optimistic
- **Goal:** To achieve a happy, simple, and pure life by spreading optimism and doing the right thing.
- **Brand Examples:** Coca-Cola, McDonald's, The Honest Company, Charmin

---

### Citizen
- **Display name:** The Citizen
- **Aliases:** Everyman · Best Friend · Regular Joe
- **Motto:** "All for one and one for all"
- **Description:** The Citizen is the brand that feels like a neighbor, not a vendor. Unpretentious, real, hardworking. They don't need to be the coolest one in the room — they just need everyone in the room to feel like they belong.
- **Strengths:** Values-driven, Accessible, Practical, Warm
- **Voice:** Friendly, Humble, Empathetic, Inclusive
- **Goal:** To bond with others through common experiences by being humble, hardworking, and friendly.
- **Brand Examples:** Target, IKEA, Trader Joe's, Habitat for Humanity

---

### Hero
- **Display name:** The Hero
- **Aliases:** Protector · Champion · Warrior · Dragon Slayer
- **Motto:** "Where there's a will, there's a way"
- **Description:** The Hero shows up when the stakes are high and gets it done. Not flashy, just relentless. This archetype attracts buyers who want to feel capable — not coddled.
- **Strengths:** Brave, Self-Disciplined, Direct, Tenacious
- **Voice:** Motivational, Confident, Empowering
- **Goal:** To save the day and protect others from harm through courageous acts and self-sacrifice.
- **Brand Examples:** Nike, U.S. Army, Greenpeace, Method Home

---

### Caregiver
- **Display name:** The Caregiver
- **Aliases:** Nurturer · Altruist · Helper · Mother
- **Motto:** "Love your neighbor as yourself"
- **Description:** The Caregiver is the brand people turn to when something actually matters. Compassionate, steady, selfless. They don't just solve problems — they make people feel taken care of.
- **Strengths:** Compassion, Generosity, Selflessness, Empathy
- **Voice:** Considerate, Thoughtful, Kind, Encouraging
- **Goal:** To help and heal others by providing for their needs.
- **Brand Examples:** Campbell's, Johnson & Johnson, TOMS Shoes, Allstate

---

### Explorer
- **Display name:** The Explorer
- **Aliases:** Pioneer · Adventurer · Seeker · Iconoclast
- **Motto:** "Live adventurously"
- **Description:** The Explorer goes where others haven't. Independent, curious, always ahead of the curve. This archetype attracts buyers who don't want to be told where to go — they want a guide for the road they're already on.
- **Strengths:** Self-motivating, Ambitious, Adventurous, Authentic
- **Voice:** Open-minded, Resourceful, Cultured, Freeing
- **Goal:** To find out who you are through exploring the world.
- **Brand Examples:** North Face, Jeep, Subaru, Starbucks

---

### Lover
- **Display name:** The Lover
- **Aliases:** Romantic · Hedonist · Pleasure-Seeker
- **Motto:** "Follow your bliss"
- **Description:** The Lover makes everything feel like an occasion. Sensory, intimate, passionate. This archetype builds deep loyalty because it connects with what people actually desire — not just what they say they want.
- **Strengths:** Passionate, Great with people, Committed, Spellbinding
- **Voice:** Affectionate, Sensory, Beautiful, Emotional
- **Goal:** To pursue perfect enjoyment through physical and emotional experience.
- **Brand Examples:** Victoria's Secret, BMW, Godiva, Haagen-Dazs

---

### Rebel
- **Display name:** The Rebel
- **Aliases:** Maverick · Outlaw · Revolutionary · Misfit
- **Motto:** "Rules are made to be broken"
- **Description:** The Rebel doesn't ask permission. This archetype disrupts industries, challenges assumptions, and builds fierce loyalty by saying out loud what everyone else is already thinking. Buyers don't follow a Rebel — they join one.
- **Strengths:** Confident, Risk-taker, Charismatic, Street smart
- **Voice:** Bold, Candid, Raw, Honest
- **Goal:** To rise up in the face of opposition and challenge the status quo.
- **Brand Examples:** Apple, Harley Davidson, Virgin, Urban Outfitters

---

### Creator
- **Display name:** The Creator
- **Aliases:** Innovator · Inventor · Designer · Artist
- **Motto:** "Imagine the possibilities"
- **Description:** The Creator is allergic to mediocrity. Everything this archetype touches has to be original, intentional, and lasting. They attract buyers who can tell the difference — and care deeply that someone else does too.
- **Strengths:** Creative, Imaginative, Visionary, Quirky, Individual
- **Voice:** Descriptive, Visual, Symbolic
- **Goal:** To realize a grand vision and create things of enduring beauty and value.
- **Brand Examples:** Adobe, Crayola, Lego, Canon, HGTV

---

### Magician
- **Display name:** The Magician
- **Aliases:** Visionary · Transformer · Wizard · Catalyst
- **Motto:** "Make dreams come true"
- **Description:** The Magician makes the impossible feel inevitable. Visionary, catalytic, deeply inspiring. This archetype doesn't just solve problems — it reframes what a problem even is.
- **Strengths:** Empowering, Charismatic, Fascinating, Spirited
- **Voice:** Expansive, Moving, Articulate, Story-Rich
- **Goal:** To make dreams come true by turning problems into opportunities.
- **Brand Examples:** Disney, TED, Make-A-Wish, Mastercard

---

### Ruler
- **Display name:** The Ruler
- **Aliases:** Boss · Leader · Power Broker · King or Queen
- **Motto:** "Control is power"
- **Description:** The Ruler sets the standard — and then meets it. Commanding, meticulous, unapologetically premium. Buyers don't come to a Ruler for options. They come for the answer.
- **Strengths:** Productive, Responsible, Logical, Confident
- **Voice:** Refined, Articulate, Authoritative
- **Goal:** To create a prosperous, stable community defined by security and order.
- **Brand Examples:** Rolex, Louis Vuitton, American Express, Rolls Royce

---

### Sage
- **Display name:** The Sage
- **Aliases:** Philosopher · Teacher · Guru · Expert
- **Motto:** "To know is to grow"
- **Description:** The Sage knows things — and proves it. Built on expertise, research, and trust, this archetype earns loyalty by being right more often than anyone else in the room.
- **Strengths:** Objective, Diligent, Detail-oriented, Zealous
- **Voice:** Dignified, Wise, Curious, Intelligent
- **Goal:** To understand the truth and teach others what you know.
- **Brand Examples:** CNN, Discovery Channel, Mayo Clinic, Barnes & Noble

---

### Jester
- **Display name:** The Jester
- **Aliases:** Entertainer · Provocateur · Performer
- **Motto:** "Have fun"
- **Description:** The Jester makes the world lighter — and people remember that. Funny, fast, and refreshingly human. It's harder to trust a brand that can't laugh. It's easier to love one that can.
- **Strengths:** Life of the party, Creates fun, Energetic, Entertaining
- **Voice:** Funny, Witty, Enthusiastic, Self-deprecating
- **Goal:** To live in the moment and lighten up the world through humor and fun.
- **Brand Examples:** Ben & Jerry's, GEICO, Taco Bell, Southwest Airlines
